Frequently Asked Questions

Product Information

How can I find out more about a product?

Detailed product information, including materials, size guides, and care instructions, can be found on each product page.
If you have further questions, please contact our customer service team.

Ordering Process

Can I order by phone?

Yes, we accept phone orders.
Please call our customer service number available on our Contact Us page, and our team will assist you with placing your order.

How do I make changes to my shopping cart?

You can change the quantity of an item or remove it entirely by going to your shopping cart.
Make the desired changes directly in the cart before proceeding to checkout.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard)and mobile money.

Returns

How do I return an item?

Please visit our Returns page for detailed instructions on how to return an item.
You will typically need to fill out a return form and send the item back in its original condition.

Account Management

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Register" button on our website and filling out the registration form.
